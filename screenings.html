Auntwo Susan

SET A Maria Andrea Gecille Arias

Aunt Susan, 54, has always lived alone and has always said she’s better that way. She’s not fond of interacting with people because she is constantly teased. Aunt Susan is diagnosed with Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD) and people brush this off thinking that it’s just an exaggeration. During her employment years, she has enjoyed the company of Julia, her 11 year-old neighbor. Time passed and a young adult Julia visited Aunt Susan - both catching up on their lives. This reunion turned out to be reminiscent and it sparked a promised they said years ago.

Beta_Test

SET B Jaye Jacinto

In a reality similar to ours, a certain company has secretly commissioned a device which ultimately detects a person’s honesty. You wear them like contact lenses and through this technology you can see if a person is lying or not. The eyepiece scans the person and collects audio-visual data to determine the honesty in his/her speech.

Close

Caramel Child

SET B Kim Timan

Set in the year of 2003, Katherine Kaye Jones, or Kakay, was a child of a call girl, Ellie Pilorin, and a missing-in-action American man. A poster of the Regional Science Quiz Bee, and her mother's frequent American customer will be the ingredients that will fuel Kakay's curiosity and determination to finally be in contact with her father, but these will also rattle the yearning child's relationship with her mother.

Close

Hahahahuhuhu…

SET B Delano Agapito

Sina Nestora, Carmen at Oscar ay nag chichikahan to the highest level sa isang coffee shop na kung saan maraming estudyante. Maya-maya lumapit ang isang kuya para hiramin ang vacant chair. Tinawag na “ate” si Nestora. HAHA ATE! Asar ni Carmen. LOLA! Bwisit ni Oscar. Then ayon sabi ni Nestora na sana sumunod nang mamatay si Oscar, pero syempre joke niya lang ‘yon. Ayaw niyang mamatay si Oscar syempre bffs sila ‘non eh.

Headline

SET A Juan Carlos Ojano

During the course of one night, a young journalist is set to finish his investigative report that will expose the private army of an influential religious group. His work is interrupted by an e-mail from his source informing him about the recent conflicts inside the church, urging him to listen to three audio files containing confessions of members from the said religious group.

Pua Iyam (Coming Out)

SET A Juan Pablo Pineda III

Some of us confine ourselves into reading books, writings and stories that shaped us into believing things, an event, or even a person. Ken will discover his true identity over a historic book that changed many lives in the past. He will confess his love to his childhood friend named Miko. But eventually, things will not fall to its place. We accept the love we think we deserve, on the contrary of how hard is it for us to accept such rejection from the people we used to believe, adore and love.

Close

A small fishing community struggles amidst an oil spill. Meanwhile, part of that community is a prepubescent and cheerful young boy struggling with his identity who finds comfort in his dreams of becoming a mermaid.

Halaga

SET B Kristine Anne Escamillas

This documentary is a story about dogs who became more than a man's best friend to their masters. The first story is about Blackjack who does tricks on the side of the streets of Espana, Lawton, and Kalaw with his master Eddie Boy. With that, they were able to earn money for their needs. The next story is about Demi and his master Mang Tony, a disabled man who has no family left. With the love and happiness that Demi gives to his master, Mang Tony was able to enjoy his life and be happy.

Close

Halal

SET B Dahren Tagtag

An Ustadz (Islamic scholar) from Mindanao, who lives in Cebu with his family, copes with keeping his Halal food business alive while thriving as an Islamic adviser in a Catholic-rich city where there is very little or no supply for Halal meat.

Close

Mama

SET A Godwin Ray Alvero

The film is about three single mothers who each has different kinds of stories and experiences that will give us knowledge about life. This is a story of a woman, a woman who is willing to do everything for her family, a woman who is ready to sacrifice anything for her love ones, a woman who dedicated her entire life just to give the whole world to her children. She’s no stranger to us, because we all know who they are and they are our moms.

Close

Panunumbalik

SET A John Peter Chua

With the very timely restoration of the Manila Metropolitan Theater currently taking place, Panunumbalik sheds light on the the many rises and falls of the historied building and how it reflects the Filipino people, the country, and the keys to their bright future. The short film features the insights and stories of four significant people involved with the theater in various points of its history.

Close

Si K.

SET B Paul Cadampog Jr.

Si K is a Brgy. Silog productions documentary that manifests a story of a less privileged boy named Kesz who started from scavenging and grew up to helping children who are less fortunate like him. At 18 years of age, Kesz has accomplished more than what most grown-ups have ever done in their existence. His passion for reaching out to the street children made him differ from children of his age. This film messages that anyone from rich to poor and from young to old may give out a helping hand to those in need.

Close

Anhedonic Nightmares

SET A Colin James Naranjo

The film starts off as an objective look on a human, exploring its parts and capabilities, slowly turning the narration into being subjective, the pronouns starting off with “they”, then turning into “he” by the latter part of the film, until it reaches a point where it talks about the character himself, his flaws, his dreams, his love for films, with the subject of being a violinist being the main point of the finale.

Close

Eminence

SET A Christian Rae Villanueva

An exploration of fame through the eyes of an aspiring actress. Her journey to her dreams, nightmares and realities. Mirroring the Millennials obsession of the perfect world they created on their screens. Inspired by Andy Warhol’s quote “In the future, everyone will be world-famous for 15 minutes".

Close

Touch Move

SET B Francis Louise Giner

Queen, the most powerful chess piece, has her own struggles in coping up with life. Until the chess piece, King, steps into her life and bring calm and purpose to protect the King. However, their allies Rook and Bishop, with the same goal, try to seduce the King into their lair of lust and love, leaving the Queen more chaotic and cynical.

Close

Sulat

SET B Jaye Jacinto

The music video revolves around the central theme of messages in different romantic events situated in different mileus. The first being at 1639 during the Spanish Colonial era wherein an indio gets attracted to a Maria Clara at the house he works in. The second story is situated in a bathroom during 1982. A college student exchanges love quotes written as vandals at the bathroom stall. And lastly, the third story is situated in 2016 wherein a girl shows up for a date in a restaurant.
